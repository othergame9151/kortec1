About Kortec Automotive

Located in Strathfield South, Kortec Automotive has built a reputation as one of the Inner West's most trusted mechanical workshops. Led by Young and Kim, our team brings years of hands-on experience to every job, whether it's a routine service or a complex repair.

We're not the typical garage. Our clean, modern workshop features advanced diagnostic equipment and a comfortable customer lounge where you can watch your vehicle being serviced. We believe in transparency, honest communication, and quality workmanship that stands the test of time.

From imported vehicles requiring blue slip inspections to high-performance sports cars needing expert care, we've seen it all. Our customers appreciate that we always explain what needs doing and why, with no surprises when it comes to pricing.

Whether you drive a luxury European sedan, a family SUV, or a weekend track car, we treat every vehicle with the same level of professional care.
